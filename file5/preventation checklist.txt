 Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist 

 Safety Tips

Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs with anyone.
Avoid clicking on suspicious links in emails, messages, or social media.
Verify website URLs before entering personal or login details.
Keep your devices updated with antivirus and latest software updates.
Do not use public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions.

 UPI / Financial Safety Tips

Always verify the receiver’s UPI ID/name before sending money.
Never approve “collect requests” from unknown people.
 Report Cybercrime
