 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts 
 
 Do’s
 
Think before you post.
Maintain respectful communication.
Verify information before sharing.
Use privacy settings wisely.
Build a positive digital footprint.

 Don’ts
 
Do not share personal or sensitive information.
Avoid posting offensive or harmful content.
Do not engage in cyberbullying.
Avoid spreading fake news or rumors.
Do not waste excessive time on social media.
